### Assistant: Sure! Here's a simple recipe for eggplant parmigiana:

Ingredients:
- 2 large eggplants
- 2 cups marinara sauce
- 1 cup breadcrumbs
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- Fresh basil leaves
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Slice the eggplants into ¼ inch thick rounds.
3. Brush the eggplant slices with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
4. Bake the eggplant slices on a baking sheet for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of marinara sauce.
6. Place a layer of eggplant slices over the sauce, followed by another layer of sauce, Parmesan cheese, and mozzarella cheese.
7. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
8. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
9. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
10. Serve hot and enjoy!
