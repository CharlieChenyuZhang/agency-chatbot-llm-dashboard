Ingredients:
- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
- 2 cups of mixed vegetables (such as broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, and snap peas)
- 2 tablespoons of low-sodium soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon of olive oil
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
2. Add the chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-6 minutes.
3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
4. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.
5. Add the mixed vegetables and saut√© until they are crisp-tender, about 4-5 minutes.
6. Return the chicken to the skillet and add the soy sauce. Stir well to combine.
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
8. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.
9. Serve hot and enjoy your healthy stir-fry!
