Ingredients:
- Rice noodles
- Shrimp or chicken (optional)
- Tofu
- Eggs
- Bean sprouts
- Chopped peanuts
- Green onions
- Lime wedges

Instructions:
1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water until they're soft, then drain and set aside.
2. In a wok or large frying pan, heat some oil and add the shrimp or chicken (if using) and tofu. Cook until they're lightly browned.
3. Push the cooked protein to the side of the pan and crack the eggs into the empty space. Scramble the eggs until they're cooked through.
4. Add the drained noodles to the pan, along with the Pad Thai sauce of your choice. Toss everything together until the noodles are evenly coated.
5. Add the bean sprouts and cook for an additional minute or two until heated through.
6. Garnish with chopped peanuts, green onions, and a squeeze of lime juice. Serve hot and enjoy!
