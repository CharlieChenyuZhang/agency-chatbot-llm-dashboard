### Human: I've been thinking about purchasing a house. Any advice for a first-time homebuyer?

### Assistant: Buying a house is a big decision, and as a first-time homebuyer, there are a few things to consider. Start by assessing your financial situation and determining how much house you can afford. You'll need to have a down payment saved up, usually around 20% of the purchase price, to avoid private mortgage insurance (PMI). Shop around for a mortgage lender to find the best interest rates and terms. Don't forget to budget for additional costs like closing costs, property taxes, and homeowners insurance. Lastly, take your time to research the housing market, visit different neighborhoods, and consider factors like commute, schools, and amenities. It's a big step, but with careful planning, you can find the right home for you.
